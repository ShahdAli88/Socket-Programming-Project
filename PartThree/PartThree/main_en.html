"Summarize of Content-Type in the HTTP request"



The "Content-Type" header is essential in HTTP to ensure that both the sender and receiver agree on the data format, preventing errors. In both requests and responses, it indicates the media type or MIME type, helping interpret and process the content correctly. If missing or unexpected, it can lead to issues in data processing.
